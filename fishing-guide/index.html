Welcome to The Snake River and Henry's Fork!

This site is your guide to some of the best fishing eastern Idaho has to offer!

The snake river and henry’s fork are known as the best trout fishing around the world. With an approximate of 5000 trout per a mile according to Idaho Fish and Game. The fishing couldn’t be any better.

Henry's fork
